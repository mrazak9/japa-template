COMPANY PROFILE

LPT JAPA Indotama stands as a leading company specializing in comprehensive service solutions for a wide range of industrial equipment, including gas turbines, steam turbines, turbo compressors, and centrifugal pumps. Established with a commitment to excellence, PT JAPA Indotama has rapidly become a trusted partner for industries relying on high-performance machinery.
